About Us

In 1940 our great father Jack Wilder, then a street artist, met our grandmother during one of these tours in a small town.
The desire to work in the world of entertainment led her to push her husband to open the Wild Circus.
The success was soon and soon more and more artists wanted to join the troupe.
Since the circus is known worldwide and still led by the descendants of Wilder
Discover their members and the show in ours fabulous gallery !
